Music

Mission:
  • To build a community of learners to have passion for music  and able to demonstrate skills of performance with confidence
At the end of the 6 years of primary school music education, students would be able to:
  • read basic music notations with comfort and confidence.
  • demonstrate skills of performance on vocal, percussions, handbells, Angklung and recorder.
  • experience performing as an ensemble and/or individual.
  • appreciate their national songs, folk songs and music.
  • perform their recreated songs with their own lyrics with the infusion of IT.
